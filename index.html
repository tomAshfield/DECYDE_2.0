Welcome to Decyde! Let's help you make that final decision.

If you have no clue what to do with your day, venture to the "Give me some ideas" side! Answer a series of questions to receive activities near you that may spark your interest.

If you already have a couple of ideas but need help making that final decision, try out the "Somethin' in mind" side. Enter your choices, then pick a random number; we'll give you one of your entered activities at random!
